It is a huge ornate structure,marked by an eight faced chhatri in its uppermost part.It was used to mark yhe entrance to the old Lucknow City.

PHONEIX PALASSIO

HANUMANT DHAM

The temple was developed by a Hindu saint Neem Karoli Baba who started by building a small temple on the banks of Gomti River in Lucknow.

CHANDRIKA DEVI TEMPLE

Chandrika Devi Temple is a Hindu temple, dedicated to one of the many forms of Hindu Mother Goddess Durga, near Kathwara, Bakshi Ka Talab in the city of Lucknow

AMINABAD MARKET

Aminabad market, one of the oldest and most popular markets in the city of Nawabs. Aminabad market is a place where you can find everything from clothes and jewellery to food and books.

CHOWK MARKET

Step into Chowk Market to experience Lucknow’s old-world charm. Famous for its traditional bazaars, Chowk is the perfect destination for authentic Lucknowi handicrafts, such as Zardozi embroidery, pottery, and traditional jewellery.

HAZRATGANJ MARKET

Located in the heart of the city, Hazratganj is Lucknow’s most iconic and bustling market, offering a perfect mix of modern and traditional shopping.

RIVER FRONT

Gomti Riverfront Park in Lucknow is a beautifully landscaped urban oasis stretching approximately 2 kilometers along the banks of the Gomti River.

LUCKNOW UNIVERSITY

The Lucknow University ,established in 1921,is one of the oldest and most prestigious universities in india.The University has a strong reputation in fields like arts,science,law,commerce and management.

SRMCEM

Shri Ramswaroop Memorial College of Enginnering and Management(SRMCEM),affiliated with AKTU and approved by AICTE.
